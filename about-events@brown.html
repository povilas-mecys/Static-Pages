Events@Brown About Events@Brown

Events@Brown is the most comprehensive place for discovering University events.

Events@Brown features a diverse collection of content from across the University community. Brown faculty, staff and recognized student organizations can add or submit University-related events to Events@Brown.

The method for adding an event depends on whether it belongs to an existing departmental calendar group or needs to go through the general submission form for moderation.

The official system for promoting events

Events@Brown is the University’s event calendar system designed to publish and promote Brown events. All members of the Brown community can use it to browse through departmental calendars and find events of interest.

Content from across the university

Events@Brown features a diverse collection of content from across the University community. The platform showcases both official University events—such as Convocation and Family Weekend—and events submitted by schools, departments, divisions, and recognized student organizations.

Events in Today@Brown

The events published in Today@Brown are a subset of all of the events in Events@Brown. Using a “Submit this event to Today@Brown” checkbox, calendar owners can designate their events to appear in Today@Brown, with a further option to designate second and third publication dates in Today@Brown.

Fully Integrated with Drupal Cloud

The functionality for displaying events on an official Brown website is made possible through integration with Drupal Cloud, delivering a user-focused and accessible design for event displays.

Additional Features

With a user-friendly interface for event editors, Events@Brown:

  • Publishes a comprehensive list of events hosted by Brown academic and administrative units, and campus groups.
  • Allows sharing of events between different departmental calendars.
  • Offers built-in RSVP functionality.
  • Displays embedded maps for event locations.
  • Allows for unit-specific tags.
  • Supports virtual events.
  • Meets web accessibility standards.
